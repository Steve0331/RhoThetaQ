Lamp Foundation

Join us as we host our Annual Swinging-4-Education Scholarship Golf Tournament, Friday, October 18, 2019 at Cypress Lakes Golf Club Golf Club, located in Cypress, TX. Proceeds from the tournament will go to the Rho Theta Endowment / Cpt. Lee Roy Thomas, Jr. Memorial Scholarship Fund at Prairie View A&M University.

The tournament format will be a 4-Person Scramble. Registration will start at 11:00 a.m. with a 1:00 p.m. shotgun start. Please register online by visiting our website at www.lampfoundation.org. Registration is $125 for individual players and $500 for teams.

If you are not a golfer, feel free to make a monetary donation or provide sponsorship for the tournament. Please forward this information to family and friends that may be interested in playing or becoming a supporting sponsor.

Should you have any questions, please contact the Homecoming Committee or send an email to boardmembers@lampfoundation.org.
