Rho Theta Monument

The mighty brothers of the illustrious Rho Theta chapter of Omega Psi Phi, are being called upon to show their love for the chapter. It has been established that we will upgrade and erect a new monument on the campus of Prairie View A&M University. It has been requested that each brother make a contribution of $200 towards this goal.
